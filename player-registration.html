Thame the Disc: Player Registration

Playing with Thame the Disc

To play or train regularly with Thame the Disc, we need to collect some information about you. For insurance purposes, you'll also need to be a member of the UK Ultimate Association, and under-18s will need a parent or guardian to complete a consent form.

Registering as a Player

To play or train with Thame the Disc, you need to be at least 14 years old, and:

Be a member of the UK Ultimate Association Enter your details on the player registration form Complete and return a consent form, if you're under 18

Get in touch

If you want to know more about Ultimate, or are interested in coming along to a training session, just drop us a message.
