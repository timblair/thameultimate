Thame the Disc Safeguarding

It is important for everyone to work together and with key services to safeguard all children. We all have a part to play and build up a bigger picture. We welcome new players aged 14 or over.

Protecting Young Players

Thame the Disc has adopted the safeguarding and child protection policy of UK Ultimate, which is the governing body for Ultimate in the UK.
